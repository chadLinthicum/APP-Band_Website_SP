Our Story

We are Smiling Politey, a tight-knit group of passionate musicians dedicated to bringing the classic hits that shaped our lives to the stage. Our repertoire spans the golden eras of music from the 70s to the 2000s, featuring classic rock anthems, grunge masterpieces, alt rock favorites, and unforgettable metal riffs.

Each note we play comes from the heart, resonating with the emotions that connect us all. We believe that music is a universal language that transcends generations, and we strive to create an electrifying experience that leaves our audience feeling uplifted and inspired.

Our love for music goes beyond performing; it’s about creating a shared experience with our fans, whether you’re singing along to a nostalgic classic or discovering a hidden gem. In every performance, we aim to ignite memories and evoke feelings that celebrate the power of music in our lives. Join us on this musical journey with the songs that have shaped us all!
